A Call for Educational Justice: From Test-Based Learning to Competency-Based Growth

Although standardized tests appear fair and objective, they often hide deep problems. They can make social inequalities worse by giving more advantages to students from wealthy or privileged backgrounds. At the same time, they fail to recognize the different talents, experiences, and learning needs of all students.
